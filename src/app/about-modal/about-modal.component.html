About This App

This app contains about 50 generated Plants and 50 generated Potions. The app is still a work in progress and a lot of potions effects, descriptions and images still need to be worked out.

Plant rarity

Plants come in four different rarities. The rarer the plant, the lower the chance of finding it.

Plant biome

Each plant has a different biome where it can be found. When rolling to gather herbs you can filter on the biomes the adventurers have travelled through

Potion strength

Potions come in four different strengths. Common plants craft weak potions and the rare the plant the stronger the potion.

Inventory

You can save your plants and potions in the inventory which can be accessed by clicking on the backpack on the right side of the screen. Right now the backpack is only saved in the browser storage. Which means your inventory is linked to the device which you are using.
